Lung Conditions and Symptoms

Disorders with Chronic Cough

  • Chronic Bronchitis: Persistent cough with mucus, shortness of breath, wheezing, chest discomfort.
  • Asthma: Chronic cough, wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, coughing at night or early morning.
  • Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD): Chronic cough, heartburn, regurgitation of food or sour liquid, sensation of a lump in the throat.

Disorders with Shortness of Breath

  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD): Shortness of breath, especially during physical activities, wheezing, chest tightness, chronic cough with mucus.
  • Asthma: Shortness of breath, wheezing, chronic cough, chest tightness, coughing at night or early morning.
  • Pneumonia: Shortness of breath, chest pain when breathing or coughing, cough with phlegm, fever, sweating, chills.

Disorders with Chest Pain

  • Pneumonia: Chest pain when breathing or coughing, shortness of breath, cough with phlegm, fever, sweating, chills.
  • Pleurisy: Sharp chest pain that worsens with breathing, coughing, or sneezing, shortness of breath, cough, fever.
  • Pulmonary Embolism: Sudden sharp chest pain, shortness of breath, rapid heart rate, cough (sometimes with blood), dizziness, sweating.

Disorders with Wheezing

  • Asthma: Wheezing, shortness of breath, chronic cough, chest tightness, coughing at night or early morning.
  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD): Wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, chronic cough with mucus.
  • Bronchitis: Wheezing, shortness of breath, persistent cough with mucus, chest discomfort.

Disorders with Fatigue

  • Pneumonia: Fatigue, fever, sweating, chills, shortness of breath, chest pain when breathing or coughing, cough with phlegm.
  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD): Fatigue, shortness of breath, especially during physical activities, wheezing, chest tightness, chronic cough with mucus.
  • Asthma: Fatigue, wheezing, shortness of breath, chronic cough, chest tightness, coughing at night or early morning.

Disorders with Fever

  • Pneumonia: Fever, sweating, chills, shortness of breath, chest pain when breathing or coughing, cough with phlegm.
  • Tuberculosis (TB): Fever, night sweats, persistent cough, weight loss, fatigue, chest pain.
  • Bronchitis: Fever, chills, persistent cough with mucus, shortness of breath, chest discomfort.
