Lymph Node Conditions and Symptoms

Disorders with Swollen Lymph Nodes (Lymphadenopathy)

  • Infections: Enlarged, tender lymph nodes, fever, sore throat, runny nose, cough.
  • Mononucleosis: Swollen lymph nodes, fever, sore throat, fatigue, swollen tonsils, headache, skin rash.
  • HIV/AIDS: Swollen lymph nodes, fever, sore throat, fatigue, muscle aches, rash.

Disorders with Painful Lymph Nodes

  • Bacterial Infections: Painful, swollen lymph nodes, fever, localized redness and warmth, pus or abscess formation.
  • Tuberculosis (TB): Painful, swollen lymph nodes, fever, night sweats, weight loss, persistent cough.
  • Lymphangitis: Painful, red streaks on the skin, swollen and tender lymph nodes, fever, chills, malaise.

Disorders with Generalized Lymphadenopathy

  • Viral Infections: Enlarged lymph nodes in multiple areas, fever, fatigue, body aches, sore throat, runny nose.
  • Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE): Swollen lymph nodes, fatigue, joint pain, skin rashes, fever, chest pain.
  • Rheumatoid Arthritis: Swollen lymph nodes, joint pain and swelling, fatigue, fever, weight loss.

Disorders with Non-Painful Swollen Lymph Nodes

  • Lymphoma: Painless, swollen lymph nodes, fatigue, fever, night sweats, unexplained weight loss.
  • Leukemia: Painless, swollen lymph nodes, fatigue, frequent infections, fever, bruising or bleeding easily.
  • Metastatic Cancer: Painless, swollen lymph nodes, symptoms related to the primary cancer site, weight loss, night sweats.

Disorders with Fever and Swollen Lymph Nodes

  • Infections: Enlarged, tender lymph nodes, fever, sore throat, runny nose, cough.
  • Mononucleosis: Swollen lymph nodes, fever, sore throat, fatigue, swollen tonsils, headache, skin rash.
  • HIV/AIDS: Swollen lymph nodes, fever, sore throat, fatigue, muscle aches, rash.

Disorders with Night Sweats and Swollen Lymph Nodes

  • Tuberculosis (TB): Painful, swollen lymph nodes, fever, night sweats, weight loss, persistent cough.
  • Lymphoma: Painless, swollen lymph nodes, fatigue, fever, night sweats, unexplained weight loss.
  • HIV/AIDS: Swollen lymph nodes, fever, sore throat, fatigue, muscle aches, rash.
