Heart Injuries and Symptoms

Disorders with Chest Pain as a Primary Symptom

  • Myocardial Infarction (Heart Attack): Severe chest pain, shortness of breath, sweating, nausea, dizziness.
  • Angina: Chest pain or discomfort, pain in arms, neck, jaw, shoulder, or back accompanying chest pain.
  • Pericarditis: Sharp, stabbing chest pain, pain that spreads to the back, neck, or shoulder, pain that worsens with lying down or deep breaths.

Disorders with Shortness of Breath

  • Myocardial Infarction (Heart Attack): Severe chest pain, shortness of breath, sweating, nausea, dizziness.
  • Heart Failure: Shortness of breath during exertion or when lying down, fatigue, weakness, swelling in legs, ankles, and feet.
  • Cardiomyopathy: Shortness of breath, fatigue, swelling of the legs, ankles, and feet, irregular heartbeats.

Disorders with Irregular Heartbeats

  • Arrhythmia: Palpitations, irregular heartbeat, dizziness, fainting, shortness of breath.
  • Atrial Fibrillation: Irregular and often rapid heartbeat, palpitations, weakness, reduced ability to exercise.
  • Ventricular Tachycardia: Fast heart rate, chest pain, dizziness, fainting, shortness of breath.

Disorders with Fatigue and Weakness

  • Heart Failure: Shortness of breath during exertion or when lying down, fatigue, weakness, swelling in legs, ankles, and feet.
  • Cardiomyopathy: Shortness of breath, fatigue, swelling of the legs, ankles, and feet, irregular heartbeats.
  • Myocarditis: Fatigue, shortness of breath, chest pain, rapid or irregular heartbeats.

Disorders with Swelling in Legs, Ankles, and Feet

  • Heart Failure: Shortness of breath during exertion or when lying down, fatigue, weakness, swelling in legs, ankles, and feet.
  • Cardiomyopathy: Shortness of breath, fatigue, swelling of the legs, ankles, and feet, irregular heartbeats.
  • Peripheral Edema: Swelling in legs, ankles, and feet, skin that feels tight or looks shiny, discomfort or pain in the swollen areas.

Disorders with Dizziness or Fainting

  • Myocardial Infarction (Heart Attack): Severe chest pain, shortness of breath, sweating, nausea, dizziness.
  • Arrhythmia: Palpitations, irregular heartbeat, dizziness, fainting, shortness of breath.
  • Ventricular Tachycardia: Fast heart rate, chest pain, dizziness, fainting, shortness of breath.
