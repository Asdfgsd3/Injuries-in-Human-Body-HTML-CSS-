Brain Injuries and Symptoms

Disorders with Headache as a Primary Symptom

  • Concussion: Headache, confusion, dizziness, nausea, temporary loss of consciousness, memory problems.
  • Contusion: Headache, dizziness, nausea, difficulty with coordination and balance, memory problems.
  • Intracranial Hematoma: Headache, vomiting, altered consciousness, seizures, neurological deficits.
  • Edema: Headache, confusion, loss of consciousness, vomiting, vision problems.
  • Coup-Contrecoup Injury: Headache, confusion, dizziness, memory problems, loss of consciousness.
  • Second Impact Syndrome: Severe headache, loss of consciousness, vomiting, vision problems, severe neurological impairments.

Disorders with Dizziness and Balance Problems

  • Concussion: Headache, confusion, dizziness, nausea, temporary loss of consciousness, memory problems.
  • Contusion: Headache, dizziness, nausea, difficulty with coordination and balance, memory problems.
  • Coup-Contrecoup Injury: Headache, confusion, dizziness, memory problems, loss of consciousness.

Disorders with Nausea and Vomiting

  • Concussion: Headache, confusion, dizziness, nausea, temporary loss of consciousness, memory problems.
  • Contusion: Headache, dizziness, nausea, difficulty with coordination and balance, memory problems.
  • Intracranial Hematoma: Headache, vomiting, altered consciousness, seizures, neurological deficits.
  • Edema: Headache, confusion, loss of consciousness, vomiting, vision problems.
  • Second Impact Syndrome: Severe headache, loss of consciousness, vomiting, vision problems, severe neurological impairments.

Disorders with Loss of Consciousness

  • Concussion: Headache, confusion, dizziness, nausea, temporary loss of consciousness, memory problems.
  • Diffuse Axonal Injury (DAI): Loss of consciousness, persistent vegetative state, cognitive impairments, coma.
  • Intracranial Hematoma: Headache, vomiting, altered consciousness, seizures, neurological deficits.
  • Edema: Headache, confusion, loss of consciousness, vomiting, vision problems.
  • Coup-Contrecoup Injury: Headache, confusion, dizziness, memory problems, loss of consciousness.
  • Second Impact Syndrome: Severe headache, loss of consciousness, vomiting, vision problems, severe neurological impairments.
  • Anoxic Brain Injury: Loss of consciousness, confusion, memory loss, motor impairment, cognitive deficits.

Disorders with Seizures

  • Intracranial Hematoma: Headache, vomiting, altered consciousness, seizures, neurological deficits.
  • Penetrating Brain Injury: Severe headache, loss of consciousness, neurological deficits, seizures, bleeding.

Disorders with Cognitive Impairments

Disorders with Neurological Deficits

  • Penetrating Brain Injury: Severe headache, loss of consciousness, neurological deficits, seizures, bleeding.

Disorders with Vision Problems
