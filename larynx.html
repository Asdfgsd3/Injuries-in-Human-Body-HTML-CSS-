Larynx Injuries and Symptoms

Disorders with Hoarseness or Voice Changes

  • Laryngitis: Hoarseness, weak voice or voice loss, tickling sensation and rawness in the throat, sore throat, dry throat, dry cough.
  • Vocal Cord Nodules: Hoarseness, breathy voice, rough or scratchy voice, vocal fatigue, voice breaks.
  • Vocal Cord Polyps: Hoarseness, breathy voice, rough or scratchy voice, vocal fatigue, difficulty projecting the voice.
  • Throat Cancer: Hoarseness, persistent sore throat, difficulty swallowing, ear pain, weight loss, lump or mass in the neck.

Disorders with Difficulty Swallowing (Dysphagia)

  • Laryngitis: Hoarseness, weak voice or voice loss, tickling sensation and rawness in the throat, sore throat, dry throat, dry cough.
  • Throat Cancer: Difficulty swallowing, persistent sore throat, ear pain, weight loss, lump or mass in the neck, hoarseness.
  • Esophageal Stricture: Difficulty swallowing, feeling of food stuck in the throat or chest, regurgitation of food, unintentional weight loss.

Disorders with Persistent Cough

  • Laryngitis: Hoarseness, weak voice or voice loss, tickling sensation and rawness in the throat, sore throat, dry throat, dry cough.
  • Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD): Chronic cough, heartburn, regurgitation of food or sour liquid, sensation of a lump in the throat.
  • Chronic Bronchitis: Persistent cough with mucus, shortness of breath, wheezing, chest discomfort.

Disorders with Breathing Difficulties

  • Laryngitis: Hoarseness, weak voice or voice loss, tickling sensation and rawness in the throat, sore throat, dry throat, dry cough.
  • Vocal Cord Paralysis: Breathy voice, hoarseness, shortness of breath, choking or coughing while swallowing, inability to speak loudly.
  • Throat Cancer: Difficulty breathing, persistent sore throat, difficulty swallowing, ear pain, weight loss, lump or mass in the neck.

Disorders with Throat Pain

  • Laryngitis: Hoarseness, weak voice or voice loss, tickling sensation and rawness in the throat, sore throat, dry throat, dry cough.
  • Throat Cancer: Persistent sore throat, difficulty swallowing, ear pain, weight loss, lump or mass in the neck, hoarseness.
  • Pharyngitis: Sore throat, difficulty swallowing, red and swollen tonsils, white patches or pus on the tonsils, swollen lymph nodes in the neck.
